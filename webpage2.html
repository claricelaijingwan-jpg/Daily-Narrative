The “Hot Cuisine Adventure” Must-try Ayam Gepuk spots in Metrocity Kuching, Sarawak!

Forget travelling, here are some top spots in Kuching, where you can enjoy Ayam Gepuk just like its origin.

     
Belbelicious
       
25 Dec, 2025

Image by Nabil Aiman


If you are a spice lover and crave something hot and nutty, you have to visit these places where you will eat your stress away. Originating from Indonesia, Ayam Gepuk has become a sensational dish in recent years. Now, many restaurants are “springing out like mushrooms”, but not everyone nailed the true essence of this dish. We’re talking about the places where the sambal is pounded fresh, the chicken is juicy and crisp, and the spice hits the spots.

Uncle Gepuk (Metrocity)

Located in the bustling heart of Metrocity, Matang, Uncle Gepuk has quickly become the go-to for the night-owl crowd and students alike.

Trendy, exciting, always busy. Located at the pasar malam, where true Malaysians go for midnight snacking with friends and family.Their Ayam Gepuk is famous for its juicy chicken, bursting with marinade flavour. Not to forget, the savoury fried cabbage pairs well with sambal and rice, alongside fried tempeh and cucumber to give a refreshing taste.Their sambal has a nutty and savoury taste along with a kick of spices. You know what else is good? You can actually ask for extra sambal, and it's free!

Gepok Bro

The next spot is located at Metrocity and recently opened their new branch at the Student Pavilion (Medan Selera) within Universiti Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS). Despite having only recently started their business, Gepuk Bro has captured the hearts of the locals with their authentic and ‘textbook’ like taste of Ayam Gepuk.

Image by Nabil Aiman


The Vibe: Minimalist and focused. They aim to give a comforting vibe and ambience to their customer with their simple design and focus more on their hot dish. The Sambal: Their sambal hits different, despite Ayam Gepuk being known for its fiery spice. Here at Gepuk Bro, you can choose the spice level. From ‘Manja’ for non-spice eater, ‘Rindu’ enough to make you sweat, and lastly ‘Berapi’ definitely burns your mouth but is still enjoyable. Big eater lover: Their set is known for being great value for money, not only that, you can actually get a free refill of plain water with any set purchase, rice, sambal, also free flow as much as you want!

Orked Ayam Gepuk

Image by Nabil Aiman


For those who prefer a touch of "home-cooked" soul in their meal, Orked Ayam Gepuk is a must-visit. This spot feels like it was made with love, and the flavours reflect that. The Vibe: Welcoming and unpretentious. It’s the kind of place where you feel like a regular even on your first visit.The Flavour: Orked’s sambal tends to have a slightly brighter, fresher note. It isn't just about pure heat; there is a complexity to the spices that makes it stand out from the oilier versions found elsewhere. Don't Miss: Their fried cabbage is always done to perfection with sweet, charred, and is the ultimate companion to the spicy chicken.

Restu Ibunda

A true staple in the local community, Restu Ibunda is where you go when you want a meal that feels complete. They’ve been serving up satisfaction for a long time, and their experience shows. The Vibe: Classic Kuching eatery vibes. It’s reliable, fast, and friendly. The Experience: What sets them apart is the "wholeness" of the meal. The rice is always fluffy, the sambal is consistently fiery, and the chicken is smashed just right to let the flavours soak into the meat. The "Ibu" Touch: As the name suggests, there’s a sense of motherly generosity in their portions. You definitely won’t leave here hungry.

Reading this post will definitely ignite the “fire” to visit all Ayam Gepuk spots! Stay tuned for more upcoming top-picks restaurants in the future. Thanks for reading!
